# Changelog

## [2026-06-14] — Castle Siege retheme

Replaced all sci-fi visuals with a medieval fantasy theme.

**Enemies**
- Space aliens → pixel-art Goblins (green, 10 pts), Orcs (brown/tusked, 20 pts), Trolls (stone-grey, 40 pts)

**Player**
- Spaceship → cannon on a wheeled wooden cart with a metal barrel

**Mystery target**
- UFO → red dragon that faces the direction it flies and breathes fire

**Bunkers**
- Neon green shields → stone walls that crack and darken as they take damage

**Projectiles**
- Laser beams → round cannonballs (player) and thrown rocks (enemies)

**Visuals**
- Neon green/blue/pink sci-fi palette → warm gold/amber/earthy tones
- Moonlit warm starfield instead of cool blue
- Earthy brown ground line

**Text**
- Title: "Space Invaders" → "Castle Siege"
- Flavor text updated to match fantasy setting

---

## [2026-06-14] — Initial release

Browser-based Space Invaders clone. Single self-contained `index.html` with no dependencies.

- Classic marching swarm mechanic with step-based movement
- Three alien types (10 / 20 / 40 pts) as pixel sprites
- Mystery UFO for bonus points
- Four destructible bunkers
- 3 lives, escalating waves
- Score + localStorage high score, particle explosions, twinkling starfield, built-in sound synth
